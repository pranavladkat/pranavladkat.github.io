About Me:

I recently graduated from University at Buffalo (UB), The State University of New York and completed Master of Science in Mechanical Engineering with emphasis on Computational Sciences. My interests are in developing scientific computing codes in a high performance framework. 

I enjoy programming and I'm well versed with C, C++ and parallel programming paradigms using MPI, OpenMP and CUDA. I also take interest in numerical grid/mesh generation.
